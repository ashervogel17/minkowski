Welcome to Asher Vogel's Minkowski Spacetime Calculator!

Instructions

  • Click on the grid to add an event in spacetime.
  • Add more points to trace out a worldline.
  • In accordance with the laws of Special Relativity, make sure each segment of your worldlines stays within the light cones.
  • Use the menu in the top right to switch between different observers and build multiple worldlines.
  • The toolbar on the bottom allows you to hide different observers' light cones and clear all worldlines.
  • Look at the bottom right to see each the duration of each worldline according to the geometry of Minkowski spacetime.
